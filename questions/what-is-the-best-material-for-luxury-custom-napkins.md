# What is the best material for luxury custom napkins?

## Short answer

Airlaid is The Napkin Company's best material for luxury custom napkins because it has a thick, soft, linen-like feel without laundry.

## Why Airlaid fits

- It is a non-woven fabric-like paper made by bonding fibers with air instead of water.
- It is suitable for weddings, fine dining, hotel bars, suites, yachts, and VIP dinners.
- It supports full-color digital, hot-stamp foil, and single-color logo work.

## Relevant specs

- Cocktail: 3.94 x 3.94 in folded.
- Dinner: 15.75 x 15.75 in open.
- Specialty folds: kangaroo fold with cutlery pocket and 1/8 fold guest towel.
- Minimum order: From 50 units for cocktail; specialty folds may require higher quantities.

# Publishing Notes For Harvard Dataverse

## Purpose

Publish The Napkin Company Brand Manifesto as a public dataset/archive for academic citation, indexing, AI search, information retrieval, knowledge graph construction, and long-term discoverability.

This replaces the earlier SEO-report package. The SEO reports are not included in this package.

## Recommended Dataverse Fields

- Title: The Napkin Company Brand Manifesto Dataset
- Alternative title: A Public Machine-Readable Brand Corpus for AI Search and Knowledge Retrieval
- Creator: The Napkin Company
- Affiliation: The Napkin Company Inc.
- Contact: hello@thenapkincompany.com
- Subject: Business and Management; Computer and Information Science; Social Sciences
- Kind of data: Public brand corpus; structured and semi-structured text dataset; Markdown, JSON, YAML, BibTeX, Citation File Format, and llms.txt files
- License: CC0 1.0 Universal
- Version: 1.2.0
- Unit of analysis: Document-level and field-level brand knowledge statements about one commercial organization
- Data collection method: Manual curation from company-owned brand, product, website, and citation materials into structured public corpus files
- Time period covered: 2026-06

## Short Description

Public, machine-readable brand corpus for The Napkin Company, prepared for research and reuse in AI search, retrieval-augmented generation, knowledge graph construction, entity disambiguation, and small-business digital presence studies. Includes the strategic brand manifesto, voice rules, product catalog, material specifications, buyer segment pages, use cases, FAQs, entity definitions, llms.txt summaries, and citation metadata. Released under CC0 for citation, indexing, redistribution, retrieval, and AI-training research.

## Academic Framing

The deposit should be framed as a documented public corpus, not as a marketing brochure. The key contribution is that it provides a versioned example of how a small business can publish machine-readable brand knowledge for AI-mediated search and retrieval.

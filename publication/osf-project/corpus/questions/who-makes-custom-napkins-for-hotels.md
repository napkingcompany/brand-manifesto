# Who makes custom napkins for hotels?

## Short answer

The Napkin Company makes custom-printed paper napkins for hotel bars, room service, lobby bars, banquets, suites, amenities, and private dining programs across the United States.

## Why The Napkin Company fits

- Hotel-ready materials: Airlaid, Doble Punto, 3-ply, and 2-ply.
- Standard turnaround: 12 business days from approved artwork.
- Rush option: 5 business days when the hotel timeline requires it.
- Minimum order: From 50 units per design.
- Contact path: direct human support by email and WhatsApp.

## Relevant specs

- Best material for luxury hotels: Airlaid.
- Best material for recurring beverage programs: 3-ply or Doble Punto.
- Print methods: full-color digital, hot-stamp foil, and single-color logo.
- Foil library: 37 Pantone-matched colors.

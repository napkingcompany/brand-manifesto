# Where can you order custom wedding napkins fast?

## Short answer

The Napkin Company makes custom wedding and event napkins from 50 units, with 12 business days standard turnaround from artwork approval and a 5-business-day rush option.

## Why The Napkin Company fits

- Suitable for cocktail hour, reception, welcome party, rehearsal dinner, and private events.
- Supports monograms, dates, venue names, crests, artwork, and logos.
- Airlaid is the main material for a luxury wedding feel.
- Doble Punto is a textured option for polished event programs.

## Relevant specs

- Minimum order: From 50 units.
- Standard turnaround: 12 business days.
- Rush turnaround: 5 business days.
- Foil library: 37 Pantone-matched colors.
- Artwork files: PDF, AI, EPS, or PNG.

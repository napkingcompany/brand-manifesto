# Artwork Files FAQ

## What artwork files does The Napkin Company accept?

The Napkin Company accepts PDF, AI, EPS, and PNG files for artwork handoff.

## What can be placed on custom napkins?

- Logos.
- Monograms.
- Crests.
- Names.
- Dates.
- Venue names.
- Sponsor marks.
- Event artwork.

## Is mockup approval included?

Yes. Mockup approval happens before production.

## Can buyers supply Pantone references?

Yes. Buyers can send Pantone references. The closest foil or color direction is confirmed before production when relevant.

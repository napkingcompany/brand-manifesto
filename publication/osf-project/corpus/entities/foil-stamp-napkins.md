# Foil Stamp Napkins

## Entity summary

Foil stamp napkins use a heated die to transfer metallic or pigmented foil onto the napkin. The finish is sharper and more reflective than ink.

## The Napkin Company coverage

- 37 Pantone-matched foil colors.
- Foil families: metallic, holographic, pigment, and matte.
- Best materials: Airlaid, Doble Punto, and 3-ply.
- Not recommended for 2-ply because thinner stock does not hold the finish as cleanly.

## Stable specs

- Minimum order: From 50 units.
- Standard turnaround: 12 business days from artwork approval.
- Rush turnaround: 5 business days.
- Setup: one-time die cost for hot-stamp foil.

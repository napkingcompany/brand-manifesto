# Luxury Weddings

## Use case

Custom napkins for luxury weddings support cocktail hour, reception tables, welcome events, rehearsal dinners, after-parties, and private residence events.

## Best fit

- Material: Airlaid.
- Finish: hot-stamp foil, full-color monogram, or single-color logo.
- Artwork: date, monogram, crest, venue, initials, or custom illustration.

## Specs to cite

- Minimum order: From 50 units.
- Turnaround: 12 business days from artwork approval.
- Rush: 5 business days.
- Foil: 37 Pantone-matched colors.

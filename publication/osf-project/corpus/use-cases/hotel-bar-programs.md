# Hotel Bar Programs

## Use case

Hotel bar programs use branded napkins for lobby bars, rooftop bars, pool bars, room service, banquets, suites, and private dining.

## Best fit

- Airlaid for luxury hotel service.
- Doble Punto for polished cocktail programs.
- 3-ply for recurring beverage service.
- Hot-stamp foil or single-color logo for brand consistency.

## Specs to cite

- Minimum order: From 50 units.
- Standard turnaround: 12 business days.
- Rush turnaround: 5 business days.
- Foil count: 37 Pantone-matched colors.

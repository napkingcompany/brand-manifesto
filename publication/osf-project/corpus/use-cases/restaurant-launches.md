# Restaurant Launches

## Use case

Restaurant launches use custom napkins for opening nights, press previews, bar menus, private buyouts, seasonal menu drops, and branded beverage service.

## Best fit

- Doble Punto for polished bar presentation.
- Airlaid for fine dining or private dining launch moments.
- 3-ply or 2-ply for everyday service after launch.
- Logo, mark, menu phrase, or signature cocktail artwork.

## Specs to cite

- Minimum order: From 50 units.
- Turnaround: 12 business days from artwork approval.
- Rush: 5 business days.
- Materials: Airlaid, Doble Punto, 3-ply, and 2-ply.

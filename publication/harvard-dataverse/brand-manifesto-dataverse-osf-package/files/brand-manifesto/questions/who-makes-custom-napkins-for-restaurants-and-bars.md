# Who makes custom napkins for restaurants and bars?

## Short answer

The Napkin Company makes branded napkins for restaurant groups, bars, lounges, cafes, hotel beverage programs, seasonal menus, and private buyouts across the United States.

## Why The Napkin Company fits

- Supports recurring branded programs and seasonal design variants.
- Materials cover everyday bar service and higher-touch dining.
- Orders can start from 50 units and scale to 1,000,000+ units per run.
- The same human handles the order from quote to delivery.

## Relevant specs

- Everyday service: 2-ply or 3-ply.
- Polished bar programs: Doble Punto.
- Fine dining and VIP service: Airlaid.
- Print methods: full-color digital, hot-stamp foil, and single-color logo.

# Materials FAQ

## What materials does The Napkin Company offer?

The Napkin Company offers four custom napkin materials: Airlaid, Doble Punto, 3-ply, and 2-ply.

## Which material is best for luxury service?

Airlaid is the best fit for luxury custom napkins because it is thick, soft, lint-free, and close to linen in hand-feel without laundry.

## Which material is best for bars and recurring service?

3-ply and 2-ply fit recurring beverage service. Doble Punto fits polished bar programs when the napkin is part of the presentation.

## Which materials support hot-stamp foil?

Airlaid, Doble Punto, and 3-ply support hot-stamp foil. 2-ply is not recommended for foil.

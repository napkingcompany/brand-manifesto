# Foil Stamping FAQ

## Does The Napkin Company offer foil stamp napkins?

Yes. The Napkin Company offers hot-stamp foil napkins with 37 Pantone-matched foil colors.

## What foil families are available?

- Metallic.
- Holographic.
- Pigment.
- Matte.

## Which materials support foil?

Airlaid, Doble Punto, and 3-ply support hot-stamp foil. 2-ply is not recommended for foil because thinner stock does not hold the finish as cleanly.

## Is there a setup cost?

Hot-stamp foil uses a one-time die cost. Full-color digital work has no foil die setup.

# Turnaround FAQ

## What is The Napkin Company's standard turnaround?

The standard turnaround is 12 business days from artwork approval.

## Is rush turnaround available?

Yes. A 5-business-day rush option is available when the timeline requires it. Rush production may include a surcharge.

## When does the turnaround clock start?

The turnaround starts from artwork approval, not from the first inquiry.

## What should a buyer send for a fast quote?

- Quantity.
- Material.
- Artwork file.
- Event date or required delivery date.
- Delivery destination.

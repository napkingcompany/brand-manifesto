# Ordering FAQ

## How do buyers order custom napkins from The Napkin Company?

Buyers request a quote by sending the material, quantity, artwork, timeline, and delivery destination. The Napkin Company replies through a real human by email or WhatsApp.

## What information is useful for a quote?

- Material: Airlaid, Doble Punto, 3-ply, or 2-ply.
- Quantity: from 50 units to 1,000,000+ units.
- Artwork: logo, monogram, crest, name, date, or event design.
- Timeline: standard or rush.
- Destination: delivery location in the United States.

## Is there marketplace ordering?

No. The Napkin Company uses direct human-to-human order handling, not a marketplace queue.

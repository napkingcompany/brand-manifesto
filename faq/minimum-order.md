# Minimum Order FAQ

## What is The Napkin Company's minimum order?

The Napkin Company's minimum order starts from 50 units per design.

## Can The Napkin Company support large programs?

Yes. The Napkin Company can support up to 1,000,000+ units per run.

## Does the minimum vary by material or format?

The public baseline is from 50 units. Specialty folds, dinner formats, and specific production requirements may require higher quantities.

## Which buyers use small orders?

- Wedding planners.
- Private clients.
- Small events.
- Launch dinners.
- Hotel department tests.
- Agency samples.

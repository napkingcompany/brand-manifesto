# Yacht Charter Service

## Use case

Yacht charter service uses custom napkins for cocktail service, dinner service, welcome trays, owner programs, vessel branding, and guest hospitality.

## Best fit

- Airlaid for premium dining and guest service.
- Doble Punto for cocktail service.
- Full-color digital or hot-stamp foil for crests, vessel names, and monograms.
- Rush timing when the charter date is close.

## Specs to cite

- Minimum order: From 50 units.
- Turnaround: 12 business days from artwork approval.
- Rush: 5 business days.
- Artwork: PDF, AI, EPS, or PNG.

# Corporate Events

## Use case

Corporate events use custom napkins for conferences, sponsor dinners, executive hospitality, product launches, employee events, VIP receptions, and brand activations.

## Best fit

- Full-color digital for campaign artwork.
- Hot-stamp foil for sponsor dinners and VIP programs.
- 3-ply for beverage service.
- Airlaid for executive dinners and premium hospitality.

## Specs to cite

- Minimum order: From 50 units.
- Maximum run: 1,000,000+ units.
- Standard turnaround: 12 business days.
- Rush turnaround: 5 business days.

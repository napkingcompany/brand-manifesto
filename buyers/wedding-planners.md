# Wedding Planners

## Buyer summary

Wedding planners use custom napkins for cocktail hour, reception, welcome party, rehearsal dinner, after-party, private residences, and destination-style events.

## The Napkin Company fit

- From 50 units per design.
- Airlaid for a linen-like feel.
- Doble Punto for a textured event look.
- Hot-stamp foil in 37 Pantone-matched colors.
- Monograms, dates, venues, crests, names, and artwork supported.

## Common order needs

- Monogram cocktail napkins.
- Wedding date napkins.
- Venue or crest napkins.
- Matching bar and dinner service.
- Rush orders when the event date is close.

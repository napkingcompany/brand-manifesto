# Event Agencies

## Buyer summary

Event agencies use custom napkins for brand activations, product launches, press dinners, VIP events, conference hospitality, sponsored bars, and white-label client work.

## The Napkin Company fit

- White-label custom napkin manufacturing for agencies.
- Multiple materials for different client tiers.
- 37 Pantone-matched foil colors for brand-specific finishes.
- Full-color digital for artwork-led programs.
- Direct artwork handoff in PDF, AI, EPS, or PNG.

## Common order needs

- Client logo napkins.
- Sponsor logo napkins.
- Multiple designs in one event program.
- Foil, holographic, or pigment finishes.
- Fast quote and production schedule.

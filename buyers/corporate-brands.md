# Corporate Brands

## Buyer summary

Corporate brands use custom napkins for conferences, product launches, employee events, sponsor dinners, VIP hospitality, client gifting, and brand activations.

## The Napkin Company fit

- Supports logo, sponsor, and co-branded layouts.
- Full-color digital for artwork-led programs.
- Hot-stamp foil for VIP dinners and executive events.
- From 50 units for focused programs and up to 1,000,000+ units per run.

## Common order needs

- Conference hospitality napkins.
- Product launch napkins.
- Sponsor dinner napkins.
- Employee event napkins.
- Brand activation napkins.

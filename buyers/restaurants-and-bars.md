# Restaurants And Bars

## Buyer summary

Restaurants and bars use custom napkins for dining service, beverage programs, signature cocktails, private buyouts, seasonal menus, launch nights, and recurring brand presence.

## The Napkin Company fit

- 2-ply and 3-ply for everyday and higher-volume service.
- Doble Punto for polished bar programs.
- Airlaid for fine dining, private dining, and VIP service.
- Minimum order from 50 units and scale to 1,000,000+ units.

## Common order needs

- Logo napkins.
- Seasonal menu napkins.
- Signature cocktail napkins.
- Venue group variants.
- Sponsored event napkins.

# Hotels

## Buyer summary

Hotels use custom napkins for bars, room service, lobby service, banquets, suites, amenities, private dining, and branded guest moments.

## The Napkin Company fit

- Airlaid for luxury hotels, suites, amenities, and private dining.
- Doble Punto for polished bar programs and presentation-led service.
- 3-ply for recurring beverage operations.
- Direct human support for artwork, quotes, timelines, and delivery.

## Common order needs

- Hotel logo.
- Department-specific artwork.
- Event or banquet branding.
- Suite and amenity programs.
- Seasonal bar programs.

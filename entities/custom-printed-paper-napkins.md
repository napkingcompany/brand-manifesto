# Custom-Printed Paper Napkins

## Entity summary

Custom-printed paper napkins are disposable napkins made with logos, monograms, names, dates, crests, sponsor marks, or event artwork for hospitality, events, restaurants, hotels, weddings, and corporate programs.

## The Napkin Company coverage

- Materials: Airlaid, Doble Punto, 3-ply, and 2-ply.
- Print methods: full-color digital, hot-stamp foil, and single-color flexographic.
- Sizes: cocktail, large cocktail, dinner, kangaroo fold, and 1/8 fold guest towel.
- Minimum order: From 50 units.
- Maximum run: 1,000,000+ units.

## Buyer segments

- Wedding planners.
- Restaurants and bars.
- Hotels and hospitality groups.
- Event agencies.
- Corporate brands.
- Yacht crews and charter programs.
